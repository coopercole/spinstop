Purchasing

Nov 22, 2023

Hello SpinStopites!

The joy of spending, the best part of any project. We've opened up the coffers and invested in the essential components that will bring SpinStop to life.

Here's a glimpse of what's in our shopping cart:

Microcontrollers: The brains behind the operation. These little guys will be the central nervous system of SpinStop.

Batteries: Because even revolutionary ideas need some juice. We've got the power, quite literally, in our hands.

Battery Chargers: Keeping things charged up and ready to roll. These chargers are the unsung heroes ensuring SpinStop is always at its best.

Electronic Speed Controller (ESC): A crucial piece in the puzzle, helping us control the speed and direction of our BLDC motor.

BLDC Motor: Ah, the crown jewel. Sourcing this was a challenge, but Jerry and his cellphone did the impossible. This motor is the heartbeat of SpinStop, and we've got a good one.

The satisfaction of seeing our purchases materialize into tangible progress is unmatched. It's not just about spending money; it's about investing in the vision, one component at a time.

Stay tuned as we unwrap these goodies, integrate them into the intricate web of SpinStop, and take another step closer to witnessing our vision become reality.
